Kevin is an aspiring fantasy writer. He's been in love with reading, writing, and illustrating for as long as he can remember. The first book series he ever wrote and illustrated, at the age of 7, was called The Adventures of Draco and Lobo. Since then, his literary and artistic interests have diversified. He has won accolades for short stories and poetry and even taught himself the basics of graphic design and self publication. Now he is a motivated member of the Byte Us Gaming team, dedicated to enriching your gaming experience through creative insight.
